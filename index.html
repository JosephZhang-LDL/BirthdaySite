Ultra Productive Annabelle-Style Study Session

By Joseph Zhang

  • Yield 1 Session
  • Time 3 Hours
EmailShare on PinterestShare on FacebookShare on Twitter

Joseph Zhang for The New York Times. Study Stylist: Joseph Zhang

Originally discovered by semi-observant student Jo Zhang, this study recipe was first recorded in the Stacks during finals week. This recipe calls for one quality study session modeled after Annabelle Xing. While you think you might have figured out the best way to study at Yale, just wait till you try this recipe to see how effective her study methods really are. Integrating the latest developments in Xing technologies and practices, this all-inclusive session is bound to change your life, and your grades.

Note: Long study sessions carry high risk of caffeine addiction. Please be aware.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of AHA or Coke Zero
  • 1 Jitterbus Dirty Chai Latte
  • 1 bar of (it's sooooo good) 80% dark chocolate
  • 1 iPad with Goodnotes installed
  • 1 white parka (because it do be cold)
  • 1 Macbook Pro
  • 1 overly heavy black backpack
  • 1 dented AACC water bottle
Location/Ingredient Substitution Guide

Preparation

  1. Find a study spot, one that ideally has other people around but is quiet enough to get your work done in peace. Ensure that this study spot has not been studied at more than 5 times in a row, and that it matches your current mood.
  2. Text one of your friends "wya"
  3. Meanwhile, sip your dirty chai and start working on your computer. You must scrunch your eyebrows and shake your head at least twice per hour to maintain peak productivity. If you don't slam your finger into your phone every 10 minutes to check for texts or the time, you're not doing it right.
  4. When you take a break, you have the choice between hugging your significant other, texting a friend, or snackpassing a boba/item from donut crazy.
  5. Make a study sheet. It must contain pretty cursive fonts, liberal use of the goodnotes highlighter for drawings and emphasis, and lots of arrows.
  6. Take a brief break to nibble on some of your snacks or steal your Significant Other's AHA caffeinated sparkling water.
  7. After a maximum of four hours of studying, close your computer and tell yourself that you can't do this anymore and that you need a break. But the truth is, you can do it, and you're killing it, and this study recipe has guaranteed success and good Karma for all your future exams. Deep down, you know that this is true, and that no matter what happens, your hard work will carry you through life and your friends will always support you. That's because a true Annabelle Study Session isn't complete without the knowledge that someone will always have your back :)
